# Reel Scripts — Healing Soil Stories

One script per story. Each reel is 15 seconds: 3-second hook, 12-second body, end card.

**Format**
- **Hook**: Text overlay on a strong visual. One line. Stops the scroll.
- **Body**: 3–4 caption lines, read aloud or displayed as text over footage. ~70 words max.
- **End card**: Static frame with CTA.
- **Shoot note**: What to film or show.

Work through these in any order. Pick the ones where you have existing footage first.

## Production notes

- **Priority order for filming:** Stories with existing farm footage first (1, 2, 7, 9, 10). Soap story (20) can be filmed any day with a bar and good light.
- **No faces needed** in any of these. Hands, land, plants, and product carry the story.
- **Silent + captions** works for all of them. Voiceover optional.
- **Aspect ratio:** 9:16 vertical for Reels.
- **End card:** Keep consistent — white background, Healing Soil logo or wordmark, "Link in bio."
