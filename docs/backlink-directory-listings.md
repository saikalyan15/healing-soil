# Backlink starter kit: free directory & citation listings

Goal: get consistent, free backlinks from directories, which also builds the
"brand entity recognition" signal flagged as missing (Keywords Everywhere
showed "Brand Query: No" for "healing soil handmade soap" on 2026-07-23).

**The one rule that matters more than which directories you pick:** use the
exact same Name, Address, Phone (NAP) on every listing. Inconsistent NAP
across directories confuses Google's entity matching and weakens the signal
instead of strengthening it. Copy the block below verbatim everywhere.

## Address: hidden, confirmed

The business runs from home, so no street address goes on any listing.
Register Google Business Profile as a **service-area business** (address
hidden, service area = India). Use the same "South Goa" / "Pan-India"
framing everywhere below — never a home address, on any directory.

## What you need for verification: just MSME/Udyam, nothing else

None of the directories in this kit require GST, a trade license, or FSSAI
(FSSAI is food, not applicable — soap is regulated as a cosmetic and doesn't
need a license unless you make therapeutic claims, which the site already
avoids). Your Udyam/MSME registration is enough:

- **Google Business Profile** — no business documents at all. Verifies by
  phone call, text, or video call for service-area businesses. MSME number
  not even asked for.
- **IndiaMART, JustDial, Sulekha** — free listings don't require GST. You'll
  just be a "non-GST" seller, which only affects a trust badge, not whether
  you can list. If any of them has an optional "Udyam Registration Number"
  field, fill it in — it's a free trust signal since you already have it.
- **Facebook/Instagram Shop** — no business documents required for a
  catalog/shop tab on an existing page.

GST would only matter if you later want the "GST Verified" badge on
IndiaMART/JustDial for buyer trust — that's an optional upgrade, not
something blocking you from listing today.

## Business descriptions (compliant — no therapeutic/skin-condition claims)

**Short (under 100 characters, for directories with tight limits):**
> Handmade natural soap from a farm in Goa. SLS-free, made to order, ships across India.

**Medium (~250 characters):**
> Healing Soil makes small-batch handmade soap from a farm in South Goa — glycerine, goat milk, and shea butter bases with farm-grown botanicals. No SLS, parabens, or synthetic fragrance. Melt-and-pour, made to order, ships pan-India.

**Long (~500 characters, for GBP "from the business" / detailed directory fields):**

## Priority order

1. **Google Business Profile** — highest priority, do this first. Free, and
   it's the single strongest "this is a real business" signal Google has.
   Set up as a service-area business per the decision above.
2. **Indiamart** — free seller listing, strong for India B2C/B2B search, good
   domain authority.
3. **JustDial** — free basic listing, still widely used for local/product
   search in India.
4. **Sulekha** — free business listing, decent authority.
5. **Facebook Shop / Instagram Shop tab** — you already have both profiles;
   turning on the shop tab with a link to healingsoil.in counts as a citation
   and is nearly free effort since the profiles exist.
6. **General startup/D2C directories** (lower priority, do after the above):
   Product Hunt (if you ever do a proper launch post), IndiaFilings business
   directory, Trade India.

## What to avoid

- Paid link packages / "SEO backlink bundles" sold on Fiverr etc. — these are
  link farms and can trigger a Google manual action (site-wide ranking
  penalty). Not worth the risk for a few dozen links.
- Directories that don't let you set the category correctly (e.g. force you
  into "food" or unrelated categories) — a mismatched category is worse than
  no listing.
- Submitting different phone numbers/addresses/business names across sites —
  breaks NAP consistency, the opposite of the goal.

## After submitting

Listings can take 1-4 weeks to get indexed/verified (GBP requires phone or
postcard verification). Nothing to check back on before then.
